Research Interests

I develop stochastic methods for geophysical fluid dynamics with applications to:

  • Uncertainty quantification in ocean-atmosphere systems
  • Data assimilation and ensemble forecasting
  • GPU-accelerated numerical solvers for stochastic PDEs
  • Wave-current interactions and boundary layer turbulence

Research Interests

My research focuses on stochastic modeling and uncertainty quantification in geophysical fluid dynamics. I develop numerical methods and data assimilation techniques to improve ensemble forecasting for ocean and atmospheric systems. Key areas include:

  • Stochastic parameterizations of subgrid-scale processes
  • GPU-accelerated solvers for stochastic PDEs
  • Wave-current interactions and turbulence modeling
